Local considerations in United Kingdom

BS 5385 compliance in quotes closes commercial tiling leads
UK commercial clients, architects, and project managers expect BS 5385 (wall and floor tiling standard) compliance on specifications. Add 'All work completed to BS 5385 standard' to your commercial quote prompt. Contractors who reference it are shortlisted first.
Checkatrade response time under 2 hours triples your booking rate
UK tilers on Checkatrade who respond within 2 hours book significantly more jobs. Use the quick response prompt to write a professional reply in under 3 minutes. Do it from your phone while still on the previous job, not when you get home.
Mention COSHH-safe adhesive use for clients with sensitivities
UK residential clients in modern builds often ask about low-VOC adhesives and COSHH compliance, especially for children's rooms and bathrooms. Add one sentence to your residential quote prompt: 'We use COSHH-compliant adhesives and grouts suitable for all living spaces.' It removes a common objection.
Heritage and listed building tiling requires specific materials - say so
UK clients with listed buildings or period properties need period-appropriate materials for planning compliance. If you offer this, add it to your specialist services prompt and social posts. Heritage tiling commands higher rates and has almost no price competition.

Common Questions

Should I mention BS 5385 in commercial tiling quotes?

Yes. Add to your commercial quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all work is completed to BS 5385 standard.' UK architects, project managers, and commercial clients check specifications against BS 5385. Quotes that reference it get shortlisted. Quotes that omit it raise questions.

How do I respond to Checkatrade leads fast enough to win the job?

Use the quick enquiry response prompt from your phone immediately after a Checkatrade notification. Generate a professional reply in under 3 minutes and send it. UK tilers who respond within 2 hours convert Checkatrade leads at a significantly higher rate than those who wait until the end of the working day.

Can these prompts help me write about heritage or period property tiling?

Yes. Use the specialist services prompt with the instruction: 'Write a short service description for Victorian and Edwardian encaustic tile restoration and heritage tiling for listed buildings in [area].' Heritage tiling commands premium rates and has almost no price competition. A social post about a period property project almost always generates direct enquiries.

How do I mention COSHH-safe adhesive use in residential quotes?

Add to your residential quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all adhesives and grouts are COSHH-compliant and suitable for occupied homes, including rooms used by children.' UK residential clients, especially those with young children, respond positively to this reassurance. It converts hesitant leads into confirmed bookings.
