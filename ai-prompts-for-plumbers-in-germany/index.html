Local considerations in Germany

Always send a written Auftragsbestaetigung before starting
German clients expect a formal written order confirmation after every verbal agreement. Use the job confirmation prompt immediately after the call and ask ChatGPT to write in formal German with Sie form. This one habit prevents almost all scope disputes.
Mention DVGW registration for any gas or water work
German homeowners and commercial clients expect DVGW registration for gas and water installations. Add your DVGW registration status to your quote and intro prompts. Without it, clients compare you unfavourably to competitors who mention it.
Use the Meisterbrief as your primary trust signal
If you hold a Meisterbrief, open every quote and introduction with it. German clients weight this credential heavily. Use the bio prompt to ask ChatGPT to place the Meisterbrief mention in the very first sentence.
Invoice as Kleinunternehmer if you are under EUR 22,000
If you are below the EUR 22,000 threshold, you must not charge MwSt and must add the Kleinunternehmer disclaimer to every invoice. Copy the exact legal phrase into your invoice prompt template once and never think about it again: 'Kein Ausweis von Umsatzsteuer, da Kleinunternehmer gemaess Paragraph 19 UStG.'
FAQ

Common Questions

How do I write a formal Auftragsbestaetigung for German plumbing clients?

Use the job confirmation prompt with the instruction: 'Write a formal German Auftragsbestaetigung for a plumbing job. Use Sie form. Include job description, materials, agreed price, and start date.' Send it within minutes of any verbal agreement. German plumbing clients expect written confirmation before work begins, and disputes almost never happen when you provide it.

Should I mention DVGW registration in quotes for gas and water work?

Yes. Add to your gas or water quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all gas and water work is carried out by a DVGW-registered contractor.' German homeowners and commercial clients expect this registration for compliance. Without it, clients compare you unfavourably to competitors who do mention it.

How do I make sure prompts use the correct formal German throughout?

Add this instruction to every prompt template: 'Write in formal German using Sie throughout.' This takes 5 seconds and prevents ChatGPT from generating du (informal) communications, which are inappropriate for German business relationships and can damage professional credibility.

Can these prompts help if I am subject to Kleinunternehmerregelung?

Yes. Add the mandatory disclaimer to your invoice prompt: 'Kein Ausweis von Umsatzsteuer, da Kleinunternehmer gemaess Paragraph 19 UStG.' Set it once in your invoice template and every document generated will include the correct legal phrase without you needing to remember it each time.
