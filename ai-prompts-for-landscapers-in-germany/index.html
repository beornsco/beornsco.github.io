Local Info

Local Considerations in Germany

  • Language: German. All formal quotes, invoices and contracts must be in German. The formal Sie address is required for all new clients and commercial customers. Some younger urban clients may prefer a slightly less formal tone, but start formal and follow the customer's lead.
  • VAT (Mehrwertsteuer / MwSt): The standard MwSt rate is 19% for landscaping services. Your Steuernummer or USt-IdNr must appear on all invoices. For commercial clients, the reverse charge mechanism may apply. Consult your Steuerberater for edge cases.
  • Handwerkskammer (HWK): Landscaping is a regulated Handwerk in Germany. You must be registered with the relevant Handwerkskammer and hold or employ someone with the Meisterbrief. Include your HWK registration number in quotes and contracts. It signals compliance and builds trust.
  • Customer Behaviour: German clients value precision, punctuality and written confirmation of everything. Verbal agreements are not sufficient. Always follow up phone conversations with a written summary. Formal proposals with itemised scopes perform significantly better than informal quotes in the German market.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI help German landscapers manage the spring rush enquiry volume?

Yes. The spring rush in Germany, typically March to May, brings a flood of enquiries that is hard to handle manually without losing leads. AI lets you generate professional quotes and responses in minutes so you capture more jobs during peak season.

Do landscapers in Germany need Handwerkskammer membership to get work?

Handwerkskammer membership is required for regulated trades in Germany, and landscaping qualifies. Having your HWK number on quotes and proposals signals compliance and professionalism to both private and commercial clients.

How do I write a seasonal maintenance contract proposal for a German client?

German clients respond well to annual maintenance contracts because they value planning certainty. A good proposal outlines visit frequency, included works, cancellation terms and MwSt. AI generates this in a professional German-language format from your briefing.

What MwSt rate applies to landscaping services in Germany?

The standard Mehrwertsteuer rate is 19% for most landscaping services. Some horticultural supplies qualify for the reduced 7% rate. Always state the applicable rate and your Steuernummer on every quote and invoice.
