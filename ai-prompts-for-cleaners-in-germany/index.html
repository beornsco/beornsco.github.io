3 Ready-to-Use Prompts for Cleaners

Copy, fill in the [brackets] with your details, and paste into ChatGPT. Done.

Quote Follow-Up Prompt
Write a follow-up message (email or WhatsApp) for a cleaning quote I sent 3 days ago. The job is [describe, e.g. weekly domestic clean for a 3-bed house]. My cleaning business: [Name]. Customer: [Name]. Quote: [amount] per clean. Be friendly and approachable. Ask if they have any questions. Under 80 words.
Google Review Response Prompt
Write a Google review reply for my cleaning business. The review says: "[paste review]". If it is a 5-star review: thank them and mention the type of clean if they named it. If it is a complaint about missed areas or standards: apologise, explain your quality check process, and offer to return and fix it. Under 70 words.
Social Media Post Prompt
Write a Facebook post for my cleaning business in Germany offering an end-of-spring deep-clean special. Business name: [Name]. Offer: [describe, e.g. full house deep clean from EUR 120]. Mention 2 things included in the clean. Tone: warm and professional. End with a direct call to action. Under 110 words.
