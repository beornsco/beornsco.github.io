Most small business owners spend hours a week writing things that should take minutes.

Quotes. Invoice reminders. Review responses. Client emails. The same messages, rewritten from scratch, over and over.

The problem is not the writing. It is the starting.

Staring at a blank screen trying to find the right tone for an overdue invoice. Rewriting the same quote follow-up for the third time this month. Spending 20 minutes on a review response that should take 2.

This is not a skills problem. It is a friction problem. And friction compounds.

"I had a template saved in Notes. I kept editing it because something felt off. I was spending more time on the template than on the actual message." That is what most people do. They half-solve the problem and live with the rest.

What we noticed

Small business owners do not need writing lessons. They need the first draft already written. Something they can read in 10 seconds, fill in the blanks, and send.

That is what Beorns Co makes: message templates built for the specific situations that keep coming up. Quote follow-ups that do not sound pushy. Invoice reminders that do not feel awkward. Review responses that de-escalate instead of inflame.

What you get

  • Complete written messages, not instructions for generating them
  • Fill-in-the-bracket format: replace the details, send
  • Organised by situation so you find the right one in under 30 seconds
  • Works without any tool or subscription
  • Instant download PDF, yours to keep
