About Beorns Co

Practical AI adoption resources for small and medium businesses. No jargon, no consultants, just guides that work.

Beorns Co creates practical AI adoption resources for small and medium businesses. Founded in 2026, we focus on one thing: helping SME owners (10 to 200 employees) start using AI without hiring consultants or learning to code.

The Problem

Most AI advice is built for enterprises with big tech teams and six-figure budgets. Small businesses get left behind, stuck between generic chatbot tutorials and expensive consulting engagements.

The result? Business owners know AI matters, but they do not know where to start. They waste hours watching YouTube videos, testing random prompts, and wondering if they are falling behind.

Our Approach

We create step-by-step guides, ready-to-use templates, and copy-paste prompts that work with any AI tool. Everything is written in plain language. No jargon. No prerequisites. If you can use email, you can use our guides.

Every product follows the same principle: open the PDF, follow the steps, get results. We include the exact prompts to copy, the exact tools to use, and the exact sequence to follow.
