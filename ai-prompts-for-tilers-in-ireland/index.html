Overview

How Tilers in Ireland Are Using AI to Save Time and Grow

Ireland's construction boom has been great for tilers, especially in Dublin where new build developments are handing over units faster than ever. But that speed creates pressure. Site managers send messages at 07:30 asking for completion confirmations before handover. A delayed response can hold up an entire floor of apartments. Tilers who can respond professionally and fast win repeat developer contracts. Those who don't lose them to the next person on the list.

The wet room boom adds a second challenge. Homeowners are investing in high-end wet room installations at a rate not seen before in Ireland, and the waterproofing specification matters legally. A tiler who can send a clear completion certificate confirming the membrane system, the product used, and the care instructions looks professional and protects themselves from callbacks months down the line. These prompts handle both situations and the daily communication that keeps a tiling business growing.

Local Considerations

Local Considerations in Ireland

Issue a waterproofing certificate on every wet room job
The wet room market in Ireland is growing fast and so are liability disputes. Issuing a written waterproofing certificate naming the system and standard used takes 5 minutes and protects you months later. Use the certificate prompt and make it a standard part of every wet room completion.
Dublin developer market: fast written confirmation wins preferred-supplier status
Irish developers managing multi-unit residential projects need fast, professional completion notices after each unit. Tilers who respond immediately with clear written confirmation get onto developer preferred-supplier lists. Use the developer completion prompt the same day you finish each unit, not at the end of the week.
Confirm the correct VAT rate before you quote
New residential tiling is typically zero-rated in Ireland. Renovation and repair on existing homes is 13.5%. Commercial work is 23%. Quoting the wrong rate creates invoice corrections and awkward conversations. Confirm the correct rate for each job type before sending any quote. When unsure, ask your accountant.
WhatsApp review requests outperform email for Irish clients
Irish residential clients respond to WhatsApp review requests at roughly three times the rate of email. Send your Google review request via WhatsApp within 24 hours of completing a job. Include a direct link to your Google review page, not just your business name.

FAQ

Common Questions

Can AI help me write a wet room completion certificate for an Irish client?

Yes. The completion certificate prompt generates a professional document confirming the waterproofing system installed, materials used, and any care instructions for the client. It takes about two minutes to fill in the details and review the output.

How do I ask for a Google review without sounding pushy?

The review request prompt produces a short, natural-sounding message you can send by WhatsApp or text after job completion. It thanks the client and makes the ask feel casual rather than scripted. Most clients who receive this within 24 hours of a good job will leave a review.

Do tilers in Ireland need to charge VAT?

VAT registration in Ireland is handled through Revenue Commissioners. The threshold for services is EUR 40,000 per year. Renovation work on existing properties is typically 13.5% VAT; new builds are often zero-rated. Confirm your exact position with an accountant.

How can AI help me keep up with new build developer deadlines?

AI can draft fast, professional completion notices, handover confirmations, and schedule update emails to developer site managers in minutes. A prompt for a quick professional update email can be sent from your phone between sites, keeping you ahead of the schedule.
