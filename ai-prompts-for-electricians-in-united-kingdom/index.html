Ready-to-Use AI Prompts for Electricians in the United Kingdom

Customer Response
Write a professional reply to a homeowner in [town/city] who enquired about an EV charger installation. My business is [Business Name]. Let them know I install [brand, e.g. Ohme / Zappi / Easee] chargers, that I will assess the cable run and DNO requirements on a free survey visit, and that most installs in their area take half a day. Mention that OZEV grant eligibility depends on their vehicle and charge point. Keep it under 120 words, confident and clear.
Quote / Estimate
Write a formal quote for the following electrical job: [describe job, e.g. supply and install a 7kW EV charger with 12m cable run and smart schedule app]. Customer: [Name], [Address]. My business: [Business Name], NICEIC/NAPIT number: [number]. Total: GBP [amount] inc. VAT. VAT at 20%: GBP [amount]. Labour: GBP [amount]. Materials: GBP [amount]. Include a note that work will be Part P self-certified and Building Regs notification issued within 30 days. Professional tone, clear layout.
Follow-Up
Write a follow-up message to a landlord I sent an EICR quote to 5 days ago. The property is [address]. My quote was GBP [amount] for a full periodic inspection. Remind them that their current certificate expires [date] and that lettings agents are now refusing to list properties without a valid EICR. Keep it under 100 words, polite but direct.
Emergency / Urgent
Write an urgent message to send to a customer who reported a tripping consumer unit and partial power loss at [address]. My business is [Business Name]. I can attend within [X hours]. Advise them not to reset the breaker without supervision, to check if the issue started after heavy rain or a new appliance, and that my call-out rate is GBP [amount] inc. VAT. Short, clear, reassuring.

Local Info

Local Considerations in the United Kingdom

  • Language: English. Formal British English is expected in written quotes and official correspondence. Avoid overly casual language in estimates sent to letting agents or property managers.
  • VAT (HMRC): The standard VAT rate is 20%. You must register for VAT once your turnover exceeds GBP 90,000 in a 12-month rolling period. Always state VAT separately on quotes and invoices once registered. Domestic energy-saving installs (solar, heat pumps) may qualify for 0% VAT.
  • Certifications and Compliance: Part P Building Regulations apply to notifiable electrical work in England and Wales. Registration with a competent persons scheme (NICEIC, NAPIT, ELECSA) allows self-certification. Gas Safe Register is separate and relevant if you work alongside gas installers. EICR certificates are now a legal requirement for private rented properties in England and Scotland.
  • Customer Behaviour: UK homeowners research tradespeople on Checkatrade and Which? Trusted Traders before making contact. A complete profile with replied-to reviews is often more persuasive than price. Tenants and landlords expect a written quote by email, not a verbal figure on the phone.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do electricians in the UK need to mention Part P in their quotes?

Yes. Any notifiable electrical work in England and Wales needs to be either self-certified by a registered competent person or notified to the local authority. Professionally worded quotes that reference Part P compliance reassure homeowners and reduce call-backs.

Can AI help me write EICR follow-up messages to customers?

Yes. After issuing an EICR with C1, C2 or C3 observations, it can be hard to explain the urgency clearly without alarming the customer. AI prompts let you describe the observation and generate a plain-English explanation with a clear next step.

How do I keep EV charger quotes competitive in the current UK market?

A well-structured quote that includes cable run details, DNO notification reference and warranty terms stands out from a single-line price text. AI can generate that full quote from your brief in under 3 minutes.

Will these prompts work for Checkatrade or Which? Trusted Traders review responses?

Yes. The prompts include review response templates for positive and negative feedback. You can specify the platform and the AI will tailor the tone and length accordingly. Responding consistently improves your profile ranking.
