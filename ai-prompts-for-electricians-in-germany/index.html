Local considerations in Germany

Formal Sie form is non-negotiable in client messages
All written communication with German clients must use formal Sie throughout. Never switch to du unless the client initiates it. The prompt templates in this pack are written in formal German register for exactly this reason.
Send an Auftragsbestaetigung after every verbal yes
After any verbal agreement, send a written order confirmation (Auftragsbestaetigung) the same day. This is standard professional practice in Germany and the single most effective way to prevent disputes about scope or price. Use the order confirmation prompt immediately.
Your Handwerkskammer registration builds instant trust
State your Handwerkskammer registration number in your introduction message and at the bottom of your quotes. German clients actively look for this. It signals legal compliance and separates professional operators from unlicensed competitors working cash-in-hand.
Respond within 4 hours in major cities
In Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin, and Hamburg, electricians who respond professionally within 4 hours win significantly more jobs. Keep notifications on and use the quick acknowledgement prompt the moment an enquiry arrives, even if the full quote takes until tomorrow.

Common Questions

How do I make sure the prompts generate formal German with Sie form?

Always include the instruction 'Write in formal German using the Sie form throughout' at the top of your prompt. Without this instruction ChatGPT may default to informal du, which is inappropriate for German business communications. Copy this instruction into every prompt template once and it becomes automatic.

Can I use these prompts to write a proper Auftragsbestaetigung (order confirmation)?

Yes. Use the job confirmation prompt with the instruction: 'Write a formal German Auftragsbestaetigung for an electrical job. Use Sie form. Include job description, agreed price, start date, and payment terms.' Send this immediately after every verbal agreement to prevent scope disputes.

How should I reference VDE compliance in quotes for German commercial clients?

Add the instruction to your commercial quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all work is carried out in accordance with DIN VDE standards.' German facility managers and commercial clients expect this reference. Quotes that omit it raise questions about your qualifications.

Do these prompts help if I am a Kleinunternehmer not charging MwSt?

Yes. Add the Kleinunternehmer disclaimer to your invoice prompt template: 'Kein Ausweis von Umsatzsteuer, da Kleinunternehmer gemaess Paragraph 19 UStG.' Set it once in the template and every invoice ChatGPT generates will include the legally required phrase automatically.
