Local considerations in Ireland

CIRI registration is increasingly expected - mention it in every quote
The Construction Industry Register Ireland (CIRI) is becoming a standard benchmark. Include your CIRI registration number in every formal quote. Clients who are comparing builders will shortlist registered contractors first.
HomeBond warranty is a major selling point for new build clients
Irish new-build clients want HomeBond warranty cover. Add one sentence to your new-build quote prompt: 'All new builds covered by HomeBond structural warranty.' Clients who understand the protection this provides rarely delay approval.
RCT compliance is expected on all commercial sites
Revenue Commissioners require RCT deductions on commercial construction subcontracts. Add a standing RCT clause to your commercial payment terms prompt. Main contractors will check this before awarding work.
Dublin and commuter belt have the highest demand - name the area in posts
Construction demand in Dublin, Kildare, Wicklow, and Meath is the strongest in Ireland. Reference the specific county in your social posts. Local specificity generates more direct enquiries than generic national content.
FAQ

Common Questions

Should I mention CIRI registration in quotes?

Yes, in the first paragraph. CIRI (Construction Industry Register Ireland) registration is increasingly used by Irish homeowners and commercial clients as a shortlisting criterion. Add your CIRI number to the opening of every formal quote. Leads who see a registered contractor first almost always shortlist them before unregistered alternatives.

Can these prompts help me mention HomeBond warranty in new-build quotes?

Yes. Add to your new-build quote prompt: 'Include a sentence that all new builds are covered by HomeBond structural warranty.' Irish clients buying or commissioning a new build treat HomeBond warranty as a near-mandatory requirement. Builders who include it in the quote remove a major objection before it is raised.

How do I add an RCT clause for commercial subcontractors in Ireland?

Add to your commercial payment terms prompt: 'Include a paragraph stating that all subcontractor payments are subject to RCT deductions in compliance with Revenue Commissioners requirements.' Set this in the template once. Main contractors and commercial clients in Ireland expect to see it before signing off.

How do I use the location variable in social posts to generate more local enquiries?

Use the social post prompt with a specific county and area: 'completed in [suburb], [county].' A post naming 'Sandyford, Dublin' generates far more local enquiries than one saying 'Dublin' or 'Ireland'. Location specificity in construction social posts directly drives the type of work you want most.
