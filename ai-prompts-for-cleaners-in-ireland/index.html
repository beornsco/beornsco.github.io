Local considerations in Ireland

WhatsApp review requests outperform email by a wide margin in Ireland
Irish clients leave Google reviews at a much higher rate when asked by WhatsApp within an hour of the clean. Use the review request prompt as a WhatsApp message. Send it while the client is still at home and the clean is fresh in their mind.
Food facility cleaning requires Food Safety Authority compliance
If you offer commercial kitchen or food preparation cleaning in Ireland, the Food Safety Authority of Ireland (FSAI) sets strict requirements. Add your FSAI compliance and HACCP procedures to your food facility quote prompt. Commercial kitchen managers will not book without seeing this.
RCT applies if you subcontract cleaning staff on commercial sites
Revenue Commissioners require RCT deductions on commercial cleaning subcontracts. Add a standing RCT clause to your commercial payment terms prompt. Facilities managers at larger commercial clients expect to see it before signing a contract.
Name the Dublin suburb or county in your social posts
Cleaning enquiries in Dublin are heavily localised. A social post referencing 'Ranelagh' or 'Sandyford' generates more targeted enquiries than a post saying 'Dublin'. Use the location variable in the social post prompt and name the specific area.
FAQ

Common Questions

How do I ask for Google reviews in a way that works for Irish cleaning clients?

Use the review request prompt formatted as a WhatsApp message. Send it within one hour of completing the clean, while the client is at home and the result is still fresh. Irish clients respond to WhatsApp review requests at a significantly higher rate than email requests sent the following day.

Can these prompts help with FSAI-compliant language for commercial kitchen cleaning?

Yes. Add to your food facility quote prompt: 'State that all commercial kitchen cleaning procedures comply with Food Safety Authority of Ireland (FSAI) requirements and HACCP principles.' Commercial kitchen and catering managers require this before signing. Cleaning companies that include it in the quote shortlist themselves ahead of those that do not.

Should I mention RCT in commercial cleaning subcontracts in Ireland?

Yes. Add to your commercial payment terms prompt: 'Include a clause stating that subcontractor payments are subject to RCT deductions as required by Revenue Commissioners.' Facilities managers on larger commercial sites expect to see this. Cleaners who include it are seen as more professional than those who omit it.

How do I generate social posts that attract local cleaning clients in Ireland?

Use the social post prompt with a specific location: 'completed in [suburb], [county].' An Instagram post referencing 'Malahide, Dublin' or 'Sunday's Well, Cork' generates significantly more local enquiries than posts that say 'Ireland' or 'Dublin'. Localised content drives direct bookings far more effectively than generic content.
