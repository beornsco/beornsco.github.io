Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Beorns Co operates the website beornsco.github.io and sells digital products related to AI adoption for businesses. We are based in Belgium, within the European Union. For any privacy related questions, you can reach us at beornsco@proton.me.

2. What Data We Collect

We keep data collection to an absolute minimum. Here is exactly what we collect and why:

DataHowWhy
Email addressMailerLite signup forms on our websiteTo send you free resources, email courses, and occasional product updates
Payment and email dataGumroad (payment processor)To process your purchase and deliver your digital products
Basic analyticsGitHub Pages server logsStandard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited). We do not use Google Analytics or any third party tracking tools.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to our email list or free course, you actively provide your email address and consent to receiving communications from us.
  • Contract: When you make a purchase, processing your payment and delivering your product is necessary to fulfill our contractual obligation to you.
  • Legitimate interest: Basic server logs are maintained by GitHub Pages for security and operational purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the purposes described above. Specifically:

  • To deliver purchased digital products
  • To send you the free resources or email courses you signed up for
  • To send occasional product updates or new releases (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To respond to your emails or support requests

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third Party Processors

We use the following services to operate our business. Each acts as a data processor on our behalf:

  • Gumroad (United States) processes payments and handles product delivery. Gumroad Privacy Policy
  • MailerLite (Lithuania/EU) manages our email lists and sends emails. MailerLite Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages (United States) hosts our website. GitHub Privacy Statement

Where data is transferred outside the EU (Gumroad, GitHub), these companies participate in recognized data transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

6. Cookies

Our website currently uses no tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, and no third party analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tools.

MailerLite forms embedded on our site may set functional cookies necessary for form submission. These are not used for tracking purposes.

If we add cookies in the future, we will update this policy and provide a clear consent mechanism before any non essential cookies are set.

7. Data Retention

  • Email subscribers: We retain your email address until you unsubscribe. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Once you unsubscribe, your address is removed from our active mailing list.
  • Purchase records: Gumroad retains transaction records as required by tax and accounting regulations. We do not independently store your payment details.
  • Server logs: GitHub Pages server logs are retained according to GitHub's own data retention policies.
  • Support emails: If you contact us by email, we retain those messages as long as needed to resolve your request, then delete them within 12 months.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an individual in the EU, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can unsubscribe from emails at any time, no questions asked.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at beornsco@proton.me. We will respond within 30 days, as required by GDPR.

9. Children's Privacy

Our products and website are designed for business professionals and are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

If we make significant changes to how we handle your data, we will notify active email subscribers before those changes take effect.
