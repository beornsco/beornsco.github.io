Local considerations in Netherlands

VvE requirements vary by building - always ask before quoting
Dutch apartment buildings are governed by a VvE (Vereniging van Eigenaren). Many VvEs have contractor approval requirements or restrict certain work types. Use the pre-quote inquiry prompt to ask about VvE rules before visiting. Clients appreciate that you know what a VvE is.
Keep quotes extremely direct - Dutch clients value efficiency above all
Add the instruction to every quote prompt: 'Write in short, direct Dutch. No warmup sentences. Lead with what you will do, how long it takes, and the price.' Dutch clients who receive clear, brief quotes say yes faster than those who receive lengthy proposals.
Aansprakelijkheidsverzekering (liability insurance) is expected
Dutch clients, especially in urban areas like Amsterdam and Rotterdam, expect handymen to have liability insurance. Mention it in your quote. One sentence: 'All work covered by aansprakelijkheidsverzekering.' Clients who see it stop shopping around.
Appointment confirmation by WhatsApp prevents no-shows
Dutch clients expect every appointment to be confirmed in writing. Use the booking confirmation prompt as a brief WhatsApp message immediately after scheduling. It takes 30 seconds and significantly reduces last-minute cancellations.
FAQ

Common Questions

How do I make Dutch handyman quotes direct and effective?

Add to every Dutch quote prompt: 'Write in direct, efficient Dutch. No introductory sentences. Start with what will be done, how long it takes, and the price.' Dutch clients approve short, structured quotes faster than any other format. A 4-line quote gets a faster yes than a detailed breakdown in this market.

Should I ask about VvE requirements before quoting for Dutch apartment work?

Yes. Before submitting a quote for work in a Dutch apartment building, use the pre-quote inquiry prompt to ask the client about VvE (Vereniging van Eigenaren) rules. Add the instruction: 'Ask briefly whether there are VvE requirements or restrictions on this work.' Clients who see you know what a VvE is trust you more immediately.

How do I include aansprakelijkheidsverzekering in my quote template?

Add to your quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all work is covered by aansprakelijkheidsverzekering (liability insurance).' Dutch urban clients in Amsterdam and Rotterdam expect this as standard. Quotes that include it remove a silent hesitation that many Dutch clients have but rarely voice.

How do I confirm appointments with Dutch clients to prevent cancellations?

Use the appointment confirmation prompt as a short WhatsApp message immediately after booking. 2 to 3 sentences: date, time, what will be done. Dutch clients who receive written confirmation almost never cancel without notice. This takes 30 seconds and saves significantly more time than dealing with no-shows.
