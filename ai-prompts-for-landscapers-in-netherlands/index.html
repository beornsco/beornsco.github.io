Local considerations in Netherlands

Dutch clients expect direct, itemised quotes - no fluffy language
Add to every Dutch quote prompt: 'Write in direct Dutch. No introductory sentences. Itemise each task separately with an individual price.' Dutch clients decide faster when they can see clearly what each part of the job costs.
LNV pesticide registration is required for professional application
Netherlands Ministry of Agriculture (LNV) registration is required for professional pesticide use. If you are registered, include your LNV certification in service descriptions and quotes. Garden maintenance clients with pets and children actively ask for this.
VHG membership is the key landscaping trust credential in the Netherlands
The Vakcentrum Hoveniers en Groenvoorzieners (VHG) is the Dutch landscaping industry body. VHG membership is actively used by Dutch homeowners to filter contractors. If you are a member, lead with it in every intro prompt and profile bio.
Confirm every maintenance visit in writing
Dutch clients expect a written confirmation for every scheduled visit, even for recurring maintenance. Use the appointment confirmation prompt as a brief WhatsApp message before each visit. Clients who are informed do not leave bad reviews about no-shows.

Common Questions

How do I write direct Dutch landscaping quotes that get fast decisions?

Add to every Dutch quote prompt: 'Write in direct Dutch. No introduction. Start with scope of work, timeline, and itemised price.' Dutch landscaping clients decide faster on structured, itemised quotes. A clear one-page breakdown wins more work than a polished narrative proposal.

Can these prompts help me write about LNV pesticide registration?

Yes. Add to your maintenance and weed control quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that pesticide application is carried out by an LNV-registered professional.' Dutch clients with gardens near children's play areas or water features actively ask for LNV registration. Including it in the quote answers the question before it is raised.

How do I use VHG membership in my Dutch landscaping communications?

Add to your intro and quote prompts: 'Include our VHG (Vakcentrum Hoveniers en Groenvoorzieners) membership in the opening.' Dutch homeowners use VHG membership as a primary filter when comparing landscaping companies. Being a member and not mentioning it is a missed opportunity on every quote.

How do I confirm maintenance visits with Dutch clients to avoid misunderstandings?

Use the appointment confirmation prompt as a brief WhatsApp message before every scheduled visit. Dutch clients expect written confirmation even for recurring maintenance. A 2-sentence message - date, time, what will be done - prevents no-shows and shows the professionalism Dutch clients expect.
