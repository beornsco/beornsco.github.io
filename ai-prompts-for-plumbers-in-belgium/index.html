Why It Matters

Why Belgian Plumbers Are Turning to AI

The Belgian market has its own friction points. Labor costs are among the highest in Europe, which means clients scrutinise quotes carefully and often ask for breakdowns. If you're operating across the language border, a quote that arrives in the wrong language can cost you the job before the client even reads the price.

Then there's the administration: BTW/TVA compliance, Confederatie Bouw paperwork, and the growing demand for heat pump installations that come with their own EPBD documentation trail. Most solo plumbers spend 6 to 10 hours a week on admin that AI could draft in minutes. These prompts don't replace your expertise. They just remove the blank-page problem so you can send professional responses fast.

Local Considerations

What to Know About This Market

Belgium's plumbing market has a few specifics worth knowing when using these prompts:

  • Language: Always confirm the client's preference before sending documents. Many clients in Brussels switch between Dutch and French mid-conversation. Having AI draft both versions takes seconds.
  • BTW/TVA: All quotes must show the BTW rate (21% standard, 6% for renovation works on older properties). Mention this clearly in every quote email.
  • Confederatie Bouw: If you're a member, reference it in client communications. It builds credibility, especially with first-time callers.
  • Heat pump premiums: Regional incentives vary (Flanders, Wallonia, Brussels-Capital all have different schemes). Use AI to draft the summary but verify current amounts at mijnverbouwpremie.be or equivalent.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do these prompts work in Dutch and French?

Yes. Each prompt either specifies the language or includes a placeholder for you to choose. ChatGPT handles Dutch, French, and German well, so you can generate client communications in the right language without translating manually.

Do I need to include BTW in prompts?

The prompts are written to remind you to include BTW details. For most renovation work on properties older than 10 years, the reduced 6% BTW rate applies. Always confirm with your accountant, and specify the rate in the prompt.

Can AI help me with Confederatie Bouw membership documents?

AI can help draft professional communications and reference your membership, but the official registration and documentation still go through Confederatie Bouw directly. Think of AI as your drafting assistant, not a registration service.

What AI tool should I use with these prompts?

ChatGPT (free version) works fine for all of these. You can also use Claude or Microsoft Copilot. None of them require a paid plan for basic prompt use.
