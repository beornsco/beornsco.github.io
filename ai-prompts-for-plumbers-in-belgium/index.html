Local Info

Local Considerations in Belgium

  • Language: Belgium has three official language regions. In Flanders, customer communication is in Dutch. In Wallonia, French. In Brussels, both are commonly expected. Always confirm the customer's preference before sending a formal document.
  • VAT (BTW/TVA): The standard BTW rate is 21%. Renovation work on residential properties older than 10 years qualifies for the reduced 6% rate in many cases. Always state the BTW rate and your BTW number on every quote and invoice.
  • Confederatie Bouw: Belgium's main construction federation. Membership adds credibility with larger clients and property managers. If you are registered, mention it in customer-facing communications and estimates.
  • Customer expectations: Belgian customers, particularly in Flanders, tend to be precise and detail-oriented. A vague quote is often rejected in favour of a competitor who provides a full itemised breakdown. AI-generated quotes with clear line items perform better here than a single total figure.
