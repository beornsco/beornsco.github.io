Local considerations in the Netherlands

Short quotes convert better with Dutch clients
Dutch business culture values directness and efficiency. A clear 3-line quote : scope, price, start date : often converts better than a detailed 2-page proposal. Use the concise quote prompt for residential jobs and save the detailed version for commercial contracts.
Your KVK number must be on every invoice
Dutch clients expect to see your KVK (Kamer van Koophandel) registration number on all invoices. Some business clients will not process payment without it. Add your KVK number to your invoice template now and include it in every formal document.
Switch to informal Dutch for WhatsApp
Email communications with new clients should be professional and formal. WhatsApp messages after the job is done can switch to informal Dutch (je/jij instead of u). Dutch clients appreciate this distinction and it makes review requests feel more natural.
ISDE subsidy for heat pump connections is a sales angle
Dutch homeowners installing heat pumps often need electrical upgrades to their consumer unit. The ISDE (Investeringssubsidie Duurzame Energie) subsidy scheme is popular. If your work connects to a heat pump installation, mentioning the subsidy context in your quote positions you as knowledgeable and often prompts a faster decision.

Common Questions

How do I make the prompts generate short, direct Dutch quotes?

Add this instruction to every Dutch quote prompt: 'Write in direct, efficient Dutch. No introductory pleasantries. Start immediately with scope, timeline, and price.' Dutch clients who receive concise, structured quotes approve them significantly faster than those who receive lengthy documents.

Should I include my KVK number in invoices generated by these prompts?

Yes. Add your KVK (Kamer van Koophandel) number to your invoice prompt template permanently. It is legally required on all Dutch invoices. Dutch business clients hold payment when it is missing. Set it once in the template and every invoice will include it automatically.

Can these prompts help me write about NEN 1010 compliance for commercial clients?

Yes. Add the instruction to your commercial quote prompt: 'Include a sentence stating that all work is carried out in compliance with NEN 1010.' Dutch commercial clients and facility managers expect this reference. Without it your quote may be returned with a compliance question.

How do I use the appointment confirmation prompt for Dutch clients?

Dutch clients expect every appointment confirmed in writing. Use the booking confirmation prompt as a brief WhatsApp message immediately after scheduling. The message should be 2 to 3 sentences: confirm date, time, and what will be done. Dutch clients who receive this confirmation almost never no-show.
